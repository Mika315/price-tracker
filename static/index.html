Astral Hotels Price Tracker
This app is for Astral Hotels only (astralhotels.co.il). Add a booking URL with your dates; we check prices on a schedule and can email you when your rules match.
Price checks run on the server using Astral’s booking flow and API. Paste the full URL from your browser after selecting dates and room options.
When is a price-drop email sent?
Default mode is only when price drops: set Price paid, the live Astral price must be below that, meet your Minimum % (use 0% for any drop), and be lower than your last successful check. After sign-in, use Send test email to verify SMTP.
